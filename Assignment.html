Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: approximatly 10 minutes
  • Prepartion: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optinal fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
